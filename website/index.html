The Traveler

A journey in progress

A mountain isn't somewhere you arrive. It's the walking — the same stretch of trail crossed enough times that you're not quite the person who started it.

I'm a CIS student documenting the climb from curiosity to craft — learning the web now, with Blender and cybersecurity further up the trail. I'd rather show the climb than stage the summit, so this is the honest record: what's finished, what's still rough, and what's only a shape in the distance.

CIS Student · Web Developer · Blender Artist · Toward Cybersecurity

Read the full story

I came to code the way you reach a trailhead — not certain of the summit, only sure I wanted to walk. This site is the trailhead of a longer Wanderer journey I haven't built yet: something made slowly, in the open, long before the climb is finished.

I'd rather a visitor see the rough switchbacks than a staged photo at the top — so everything here is marked honestly: finished, still rough, or only a shape in the distance.
