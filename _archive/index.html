About

The Traveler

N

I'm Khaylub Thompson-Calvin, a CIS student documenting the climb from curiosity to craft. For now I'm learning the web; Blender and 3D are further up the trail, and I'm in no hurry to rush them.

A mountain isn't somewhere you arrive. It's the walking — the same stretch of trail crossed enough times that you're not quite the same person who started it. Khaylub.com is where I keep that record honestly: what's finished, what's still rough, and what's only a shape in the distance. It's the trailhead of a longer Wanderer journey I haven't built yet. I'd rather show the climb than stage the summit.
